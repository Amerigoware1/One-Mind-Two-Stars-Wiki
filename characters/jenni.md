# {{ page.title }}

## Overview
Jenni Jo Carisco is the older of Celectra’s twin daughters and one of the most emotionally attuned young listeners of her generation. Bright, intuitive, and deeply bonded to Ripple, Jenni experiences resonance as a *feeling* before she understands it as a pattern. Her early childhood is shaped by the Four Rivers crisis, and by Book 5 she emerges as a stabilizing force within the Resonance Stewardship Program.

## Physical Description
Jenni has Celectra’s expressive eyes and Jaxon’s warm smile. She moves with a soft, instinctive awareness of her surroundings, often leaning into resonance rather than analyzing it. Her posture is relaxed, her gestures open, and her expressions vivid.

## Personality & Skills
Jenni is defined by:

- emotional intelligence  
- instinctive resonance sensitivity  
- warmth and openness  
- a protective streak toward Ripple  
- a tendency to “feel first, think second”  

Her strengths include:

- early detection of emotional-harmonic shifts  
- calming pulses during resonance instability  
- intuitive understanding of Galinstanian fields  
- strong interpersonal bonds  

## Relationships
- **Xara Zo Carisco** — twin sister, intellectual complement  
- **Ripple** — closest friend, emotional partner  
- **Celectra & Jaxon** — parents  
- **Zoë & Amerigo** — grandparents  
- **Xan Carisco** — uncle and mentor  
- **Chime, Stan, Harbor** — Galinstanian elders she respects  

## Story Overview (Non-Spoiler)
Jenni’s arc centers on emotional resonance — understanding what others feel, and learning how to stabilize harmonic fields through empathy. She becomes one of the first children to work formally with Galinstanians in the Stewardship Program.
