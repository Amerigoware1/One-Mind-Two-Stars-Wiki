Overview
Sensei Onishi Kisa is the head martial arts instructor at New Eden Colony's Sundome Karate Dojo and a respected member of the Athletics Committee. Hailing from Okinawa, Japan—the birthplace of karate—Sensei Kisa brings centuries of martial tradition to Ares while adapting them to the colony's unique 0.9g environment. Known for their wisdom, fairness, and advocacy for all athletes, they played a crucial role in supporting Celectra Carisco's participation in competitive karate despite her unique dual-body physiology.
Physical Description
Sensei Kisa carries themselves with the quiet authority of a lifetime dedicated to martial discipline. They have silver-streaked black hair kept in a neat, practical cut, sharp observant eyes, and a posture that remains perfectly aligned whether standing, sitting, or moving. They favor traditional gi attire in muted tones, always immaculately pressed, with their black belt worn with understated pride. Their hands are calloused from decades of practice, and they move with economical grace—every gesture intentional, every step grounded.
Personality & Skills
Sensei Kisa is defined by:
principled fairness and ethical clarity
deep respect for individual dignity
adaptive teaching that honors tradition while embracing change
quiet wisdom that guides without commanding
commitment to excellence through discipline, not natural advantage
Their strengths include:
martial arts instruction adapted to 0.9g gravity
sports psychology and mental resilience training
advocacy for inclusive competition policies
meditative practices that complement physical technique
Their weaknesses include:
occasional frustration with bureaucratic delays
reluctance to compromise core values, even for expediency
carrying the emotional weight of students' struggles
Relationships
Celectra Carisco — respected student and advocate relationship; Sensei Kisa sees past Celectra's unique physiology to her dedication and skill
Councilor Michael Okafor — professional counterpart on the Athletics Committee; principled debates that ultimately strengthened colony policy
Yuki Sato, Kai Vasquez, Chen Wei — standout students who embody different aspects of martial excellence
Athletics Committee — respected voice for fair competition and athlete wellbeing
New Eden Youth — mentor to generations of Ares-born children learning discipline, focus, and respect
Story Overview (Non-Spoiler)
Sensei Kisa's arc is about preserving tradition while embracing change—teaching that excellence isn't about conforming to a single standard, but about honoring each individual's path while maintaining rigorous discipline. Their famous statement, "She is who she is," became a touchstone for discussions about accommodating difference while maintaining excellence in New Eden. They represent the quiet, steady force that helps a colony grow not just stronger, but wiser.
